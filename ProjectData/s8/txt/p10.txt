Having slain Monohar, the prince and Rahuti travelled to a distant kingdom. Tired and thirsty, Madan wished to rest. While he waited, Rahuti went off in search of water.  

Meanwhile, a woman named Malini appeared where Madan waited. Pretending that she would help quench his thirst, she gave him a necklace to wear around his neck. Immediately Madan changed into a sheep, that Malini took home with her.
